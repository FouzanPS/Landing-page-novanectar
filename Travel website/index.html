Destination Guides

Paris

Paris, the "City of Light," is a dream destination known for its iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Notre-Dame Cathedral. Stroll along the romantic Seine River, visit world-renowned museums like the Louvre, and savor delicious French cuisine in charming bistros. Paris captivates with its rich history, elegant architecture, and vibrant cultural scene. A perfect blend of old-world charm and modern sophistication, Paris offers an unforgettable experience.

Tokiyo

Tokyo, Japan's bustling capital, is a dynamic city where tradition meets futuristic innovation. Explore historic temples and shrines, shop in cutting-edge districts like Shibuya and Shinjuku, and enjoy a variety of Japanese cuisine, from sushi to ramen. Experience the tranquil beauty of cherry blossoms in spring and the vibrant neon lights at night. Tokyo's blend of ancient culture and modern marvels creates a unique and thrilling urban adventure.

Bali

Bali, Indonesia's paradise island, is renowned for its stunning beaches, lush rice terraces, and vibrant culture. Relax on the pristine shores of Seminyak, explore the spiritual heart of Ubud, and witness breathtaking sunsets at Tanah Lot temple. Enjoy activities like surfing, diving, and yoga retreats. Bali's natural beauty, rich traditions, and warm hospitality make it a haven for both adventure seekers and those looking to unwind.

Travel Itineraries

Paris Plan

Day 1: Arrival and Eiffel Tower

  • Morning: Arrive in Paris and check into your hotel.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Eiffel Tower. Take an elevator ride to the top for stunning views of the city.
  • Evening: Enjoy a dinner cruise on the Seine River.
Day 2: Louvre Museum and Notre-Dame
  • Morning: Explore the Louvre Museum. Don't miss the Mona Lisa and other famous artworks.
  • Afternoon: Walk to the Île de la Cité and visit the Notre-Dame Cathedral.
  • Evening: Dine at a classic French bistro.
Day 3: Montmartre and Sacré-Cœur
  • Morning: Wander through the artistic neighborhood of Montmartre. Visit the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.
  • Afternoon: Enjoy a leisurely lunch at a café and explore local shops and galleries.
  • Evening: Watch a cabaret show at the Moulin Rouge.
Day 4: Champs-Élysées and Arc de Triomphe
  • Morning: Stroll along the Champs-Élysées, one of the most famous avenues in the world.
  • Afternoon: Climb to the top of the Arc de Triomphe for panoramic views of Paris.
  • Evening: Have a romantic dinner at a rooftop restaurant with views of the city.
Day 5: Versailles and Gardens
  • Morning: Take a day trip to the Palace of Versailles. Tour the opulent palace rooms.
  • Afternoon: Explore the expansive gardens and the Grand Trianon.
  • Evening: Return to Paris and have a quiet dinner at your hotel.
Day 6: Le Marais and Shopping
  • Morning: Visit the trendy Le Marais district. Explore boutiques and galleries.
  • Afternoon: Have lunch at a local café and continue shopping in the area.
  • Evening: Enjoy dinner at a Michelin-starred restaurant.
Day 7: Departure
  • Morning: Relax at a local park or café.
  • Afternoon: Do some last-minute shopping or sightseeing.
  • Evening: Head to the airport for your departure.

Tokyo Exploring

Day 1: Arrival and Shibuya

  • Morning: Arrive in Tokyo and check into your hotel.
  • Afternoon: Visit the bustling Shibuya Crossing. Explore the nearby shops and cafes.
  • Evening: Dine at an izakaya (Japanese pub) in Shibuya.
Day 2: Asakusa and Akihabara
  • Morning: Visit the historic Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa. Wander through Nakamise Street for souvenirs.
  • Afternoon: Head to Akihabara, Tokyo’s electric town. Discover anime, manga, and electronic shops.
  • Evening: Enjoy a meal at a themed restaurant in Akihabara.
Day 3: Tsukiji Market and Ginza
  • Morning: Explore the Tsukiji Outer Market. Try fresh sushi and seafood.
  • Afternoon: Walk to the upscale Ginza district. Shop at luxury stores and visit art galleries.
  • Evening: Experience a traditional tea ceremony.
Day 4: Ueno and Yanaka
  • Morning: Visit Ueno Park and the Tokyo National Museum.
  • Afternoon: Explore the Yanaka neighborhood, known for its traditional charm and old Tokyo atmosphere.
  • Evening: Dine at a local restaurant in Ueno.
Day 5: Odaiba and Roppongi
  • Morning: Head to the Odaiba district. Visit teamLab Borderless, a digital art museum.
  • Afternoon: Relax on Odaiba’s beach and enjoy the views of Rainbow Bridge.
  • Evening: Experience Tokyo’s nightlife in Roppongi. Enjoy dinner and visit a bar or club.
Day 6: Day Trip to Hakone
  • Morning: Take a day trip to Hakone. Ride the cable car for views of Mount Fuji.
  • Afternoon: Relax in an onsen (hot spring) and visit the Hakone Open-Air Museum.
  • Evening: Return to Tokyo and have a quiet dinner at your hotel.
Day 7: Shinjuku and Departure
  • Morning: Explore Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building for a free observation deck view.
  • Evening: Have a farewell dinner in Shinjuku before heading to the airport for your departure.

Bali Adventure

Day 1: Arrival and Seminyak

  • Morning: Arrive in Bali and check into your hotel in Seminyak.
  • Afternoon: Relax on Seminyak Beach. Explore nearby shops and cafes.
  • Evening: Enjoy a beachside dinner and watch the sunset.
Day 2: Ubud and Monkey Forest
  • Morning: Travel to Ubud and visit the Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary.
  • Afternoon: Explore Ubud's art markets and galleries.
  • Evening: Dine at a traditional Balinese restaurant.
Day 3: Tegallalang Rice Terraces and Tirta Empul
  • Morning: Visit the Tegallalang Rice Terraces. Take a scenic walk and enjoy the views.
  • Afternoon: Head to Tirta Empul Temple, known for its holy spring water.
  • Evening: Return to Ubud for dinner and a cultural dance performance.
Day 4: Nusa Penida Day Trip
  • Morning: Take a boat to Nusa Penida. Visit Kelingking Beach and Angel's Billabong.
  • Afternoon: Explore Crystal Bay for snorkeling and relaxation.
  • Evening: Return to Bali and have dinner in Seminyak.
Day 5: Uluwatu and Jimbaran
  • Morning: Visit Uluwatu Temple, perched on a cliff overlooking the ocean.
  • Afternoon: Relax at a beach club in Uluwatu.
  • Evening: Enjoy a seafood dinner on Jimbaran Beach.
Day 6: Mount Batur Sunrise Trek
  • Early Morning: Start a sunrise trek up Mount Batur.
  • Morning: Reach the summit and enjoy the sunrise views.
  • Afternoon: Relax at a hot spring near the base of the volcano.
  • Evening: Return to your hotel and rest.
Day 7: Departure
  • Morning: Spend your last morning at the beach or pool.
  • Afternoon: Do some last-minute shopping or sightseeing.
  • Evening: Head to the airport for your departure.
